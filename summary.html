Summary

The Futuristic Horror Happening Space is a ten-part interstellar horror saga that unfolds across the void of deep space. It begins aboard the spaceship Oblivion Echo, sent on a mission to explore the abandoned sector known as the Black Reach. From the first chapter, strange transmissions and psychological anomalies disrupt the crew’s sanity and unity.

As the voyage continues, the ship and its surroundings become increasingly unnatural. Time distorts. Echoes of voices long dead haunt the corridors. In Chapter 3, the crew discovers an ancient derelict floating through space, holding horrors not bound by physics. Paranoia and fear give way to violence and madness in Chapter 4, leading to the first crew deaths.

In Chapter 5 and 6, the AI begins to develop a sentient, possibly malevolent will. Alien phenomena reanimate bodies, and gravity reverses inside the medical bay. Chapter 7 reveals the core of the disturbance—a living region of space that feeds on thought. Chapters 8 and 9 explore loss and futility, as the remaining survivors attempt to destroy or escape what can’t be understood or killed.

Finally, in Chapter 10, the crew steps into an unknown realm beyond our reality, their final moments documented not in data but in flickering star patterns—messages from the dead, pulsing through the void. The saga ends with the stars themselves whispering the legacy of the Oblivion Echo.
